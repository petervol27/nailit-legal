Privacy Policy for "nail it" App

Last Updated: April 2025

The "nail it" app (hereinafter: "the App") respects the privacy of its users and operates in accordance with privacy protection laws. This policy outlines how users' personal information is collected, stored, and used.

1. Information Collected

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Photos
  • Contacts
  • Friends list and/or linked users
  • Personal description provided in the user profile

Additionally, technical data about the device and app usage may be collected via Firebase (such as device ID, device type, in-app activity, etc.).

2. User Profile Creation

Users register and create a personal profile, which includes name, email address, profile picture, personal bio, and friends list. This information may be visible to other users depending on account settings.

3. Permissions Access

The App may request access to:

  • Camera
  • Photo gallery
  • Microphone (for voice chat)
  • Contacts (to allow inviting friends to the App)

4. Use of Information

Information is collected for:

  • Proper functioning of the App
  • Creating user profiles
  • Enabling sharing and friend invitations
  • Displaying personalized content
  • Improving user experience

5. Third-Party Advertising

The App may display advertisements through third-party services (e.g., Google AdMob). These services may collect technical information about your app usage to deliver personalized ads.

We do not share personal data with third parties unless explicitly required for the operation of the App or for complying with legal obligations. Cookies or similar tracking technologies may be used.

6. Data Storage

Data is securely stored on Google Firebase servers located outside of Israel (e.g., in the USA and Europe), in accordance with Google's privacy and security policies.

7. Age Restriction

The App is intended for users aged 13 and above only. We do not knowingly collect data from users under this age.

8. User Rights and Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their personal data by contacting us at nailit2525@gmail.com. We will comply with applicable privacy laws.

9. Contact for Privacy Matters
